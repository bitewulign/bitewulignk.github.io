Prospective Students & Collaborators

I occasionally have openings for students, research interns, and collaborators. If you're interested, this page explains what I work on and how to reach out.

What I look for

  • Curiosity and strong problem-formulation skills
  • Comfort with Python and reproducible experiments
  • Experience with deep learning (PyTorch/TensorFlow) is a plus
  • Interest in medical imaging, computer vision, or physiological signals

Good email checklist

  1. 1–2 sentences: why you’re reaching out
  2. 1 paragraph: what topic excites you (be specific)
  3. Links: CV + GitHub + 1–2 best projects/papers
  4. Your availability (dates/timezone)

Suggested subject lines

  • Prospective MSc/PhD: [Topic] + [Your Name]
  • Research internship: [Topic] + [Your Name]
  • Collaboration: [Problem] + [Your Group]

Contact

Email: your.email@domain.com (please include your CV and links).
